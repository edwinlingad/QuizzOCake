QuizzOCake Overview

Guide to QuizzOCake Speak

Quizz - It's a study set comprising of test questions, flashcards and reviewer notes. Users may search for available Quizzes or make their own. Parents may assign these to their Quizzlings and Educators may put suggested Quizzes in the Lessons they make in their Quizzrooms

Quizzer - QuizzOCake community member

Quizzling - Quizzers who are below 16 years old. Only parents with their own QuizzOCake account may set up accounts for Quizzlings

Quizzmate - A Quizzer you know and trust. You or the Quizzer accepted the invitation sent out to be each other's Quizzmates

QuizzOCake - A online learning tool for learners , parents and educators that provides a platform for studying, teaching, knowledge sharing and collaboration

Quizzroom - An online classroom with a teacher/moderator and enrolled learners. A Quizzroom may have lessons and content (video, text, images, Quizzes) related to the lessons. It also allows discussions among the Quizzroom members.

Safety and Security

QuizzOCake promotes knowledge sharing and while that means exposure to public users, we don't compromise on safety and security.

QuizzOCake safety features include the following:

  • Quizzlings (Learners below 16 years old) need parents to sign them up for an account.
  • Parents have the option to receive notifications when:
    • A Quizzling creates a new Quizz
    • A Quizzling makes a Quizz available to the public.
    • A Quizz created by a Quizzling receives a comment
    • A Quizzling posts a comment
    • A Quizzling's posted comment is flagged as inappropriate
    • A Quizzling's question is flagged ( incorrect answer, inappropriate, etc)
    • A Quizzling sends a message
    • A Quizzling receives a message
    • A Quizzling receives a Quizzmate request
    • A Quizzling accepts a Quizzmate request
  • Users can flag inappropriate quizzes and comments and QuizzOCake moderators can remove or hide flagged content as necessary.
  • QuizzOCake moderators have the option to suspend or expel troublemakers.
  • Quizzroom moderators or teachers can choose to accept or drop learners from their Quizzroom.
  • Private conversations with Quizzlings are not possible. Parents can view all conversation threads that include their Quizzlings.
  • Parents can see all activities in the Quizzrooms their children are enrolled in.

How Tos

*Note: Only users 16 years old and above are allowed to set up an account. If setting up an account for a user below 16, the parent or legal guardian must first sign up using these steps and then set up a Quizzling account for the user after signing in. See How to create a Quizzling account.

  1. Go to quizzocake.com.
  2. If signed in as a guest, select "sign in ->" in the header. Proceed to step 4.
  3. If signed in with a different account, press the username in the header, select "-> Log Out" from the drop box, confirm by pressing "Yes" and proceed to step 4. For devices with small screens, select the icon with the 3 bars in the upper left part of the screen. The red "Log Out" button is at the bottom of the menu.
  4. If not signed in, press the orange "Sign Up" button at the top part of the home page.
  5. Select sign up method: using Facebook account, Google account or regular sign up (blue "SIGN UP" button at the bottom of the dialog box)
  6. By signing up or signing in, you should have read and agreed with the "Terms and Conditions".
  7. If you selected Facebook or Google sign up methods, you will be asked to input your account information.
  8. If you selected regular sign up method, input your first name, last name, username, password, valid email address, and birth date. Read the "Terms and Conditions" and tick the "I agree with the Terms and Conditions" checkbox. Press "Sign Up".
  9. Verify the account by checking the mail sent by the website to the email address used during account sign up. Follow the steps in the said email.
