About QuizzOCake

Whether you're teaching, reviewing for exams or learning something new, let QuizzOCake be your partner in education.

It's is for Everybody

Learners can search and take Quizzes, make their own, join Quizzrooms and connect with their Quizzmates.

Parents can make quizzes, post assignments and track all of their Quizzlings' activities.

Educators can invite learners to their Quizzrooms, prepare lessons, make announcements & quizzes, and moderate discussions in these virtual classrooms.

QuizzOCake is for Learning

Quizzes

Anybody can make their own Quizz or search for available quizzes in different subjects and grade levels. Take the quizzes as many times as needed to master the lessons.

Notes

Use reviewer notes to study before taking the test. All of your reference materials can be written in notes so they'll be more accessible.

Flashcards

Test yourself without taking scores with flashcards. Use existing questions from the quiz or add new ones. Perfect for drills and memorization.

Assignments

Parents can assign Quizzes to their Quizzlings and set target scores and due dates. With Assignments, the parents can track and list focus areas.

Test Results

See previous quizzes taken, their scores and learn from past mistakes. You'll also see from subsequent scores how well you're progressing.

QuizzOCake is for Building a Community of Learners

Quizz Sharing

QuizzOCake is primarily a knowledge sharing website. Publishing your Quizz would help build the repository of available Quizzes and would also encourage others to share.

Quizzrooms

A virtual classroom that supplements or could substitute for the traditional method of teaching. The teacher, course materials and practice Quizz sets are more accessible to the learners and it also facilitates discussion among the Quizzroom members.

Quizzmates

Invite friends, parents or Quizzlings to connect with you through QuizzOCake. Being connected allows better exchange of ideas and information with a Quizzmate or a group of Quizzmates.
